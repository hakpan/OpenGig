About OpenGig

A place where Musicians and Venues can connect with each other. Musicians can signup to OpenGig by adding a user name, creating a password, adding their website, entering their location, and selecting what generes they play. Musicians can also link their youTube videos. After signing up Musicians can search for venues that are hiring. They are able to connect right with the people who are looking for them.
Venues

Venues can register their business on OpenGig so that they can connect directly with Musicians. Inturn Musicians know that the venues on OpenGig are actively looking for talent and they can reach out to the Venues as well.
